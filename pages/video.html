Short Video Assignment by Dor Rondel

Do Animals Like Music

Description

The video presented by Trace Dominguez on behalf of DiscoveryNews attacks the question of whether or not animals like music. The argument focuses on birds and whales, both of which are harmonically inclined. Birds display rythm and percussion patterns similar to those of jazz musicisians; whereas, humpback whales use a format similar to that of the Beetles. Additionally, the argument includes an English study which concludes that cows milk 3% more efficiently while being stimulated by melodies which are under 100BPM. Dominguez ends off saying that music connects both hemispheres of the brain, attaching meaning to sound, much like animals. This leads him to the conclusion that harmonics are universal.
